Guild Headquarters

Welcome, champion! We're thrilled to have you with us in the Guild Foundation of Summoners Rift. Our cherished Summoners Rift was once a haven of peace and harmony, a realm where magical energies coexisted in balance. However, everything changed when a mysterious rune opened a portal to the forces of evil. Malevolent beasts from other universes emerged, wreaking havoc and shattering the tranquility that once defined our sacred land. In response to this dire threat, our foundation was established. Our mission is clear: to eliminate the evil beasts that now roam the once serene forests of Summoners Rift and restore it to its former glory. The destructive consequences of the portal's opening are evident, with the landscape scarred and the very essence of our realm at stake. Join us in our quest to safeguard Summoners Rift, as we strive to create a safe haven for all. Together, let's embark on a journey to purge the malevolent forces, reclaim our land, and bring back the peace and prosperity that once graced Summoners Rift. As a champion, your courage and skills are invaluable to our cause. Welcome to the fight for the restoration of Summoners Rift!
